FIELD GUIDE

How to save the snacks

  1. Choose a defense. Build mode lasts for one placement. Click the active card again or press Esc to cancel.
  2. Place it on grass. Towers can’t block the dusty dash trail.
  3. Start the wave. Defeated cheetahs drop coins for more towers.
  4. Watch the portal. Every 5th wave tears it open for a powerful alpha cheetah.
  5. Improve your crew. Tap a placed capy to upgrade it through level 3 or sell it for a partial refund.
  6. Reposition freely. Drag a placed capy to another clear patch of grass. Invalid drops snap back.
  7. Trap the trail. Basket Snares go directly on the road, briefly stopping nearby cheetahs before recharging.
  8. Keep it moving. Turn on Auto to begin each new wave after a short countdown.
